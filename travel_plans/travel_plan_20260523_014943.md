## Itinerary
Based on the provided information, I've planned a detailed 10-day Bali backpacking trip for you.

**Best Flight Suggestion:**
Considering the options, I recommend taking the IndiGo flight from Raja Sansi International Airport to Chhatrapati Shivaji International (Sahar International) Airport, and then booking a connecting flight to Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali. This will be a more affordable and convenient option.

**Best Hotel Suggestion:**
For accommodation, I suggest booking the Uluwatu Surf Villas, which offers a great balance of comfort, location, and price. Alternatively, you can consider staying at Hotel Tugu Bali in Canggu, which has a unique collection of Indonesian antiquities and a romantic atmosphere.

**Day-wise Itinerary:**

Day 1: Arrival in Bali

* Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport
* Check-in to your hotel and freshen up
* Explore the nearby area, such as Kuta Beach or Seminyak
* Enjoy a traditional Balinese dinner and watch a cultural performance

Day 2: South Bali

* Visit the famous Uluwatu Temple and watch the Kecak Fire Dance
* Explore the beautiful beaches of Nusa Dua and Tanjung Benoa
* Enjoy water sports, such as snorkeling or surfing
* Return to your hotel and relax

Day 3: Ubud

* Drive to Ubud (approximately 1.5 hours) and check-in to a local hotel
* Visit the Ubud Monkey Forest and explore the town's art market
* Enjoy a traditional Balinese lunch and try some local cuisine
* Watch a traditional dance performance at the Ubud Royal Palace

Day 4: Ubud

* Visit the Tegalalang Rice Terrace and learn about Balinese agriculture
* Explore the nearby villages and try some local coffee
* Visit the Ubud Art Museum and learn about Balinese art and culture
* Enjoy a relaxing evening in Ubud

Day 5: Nusa Penida

* Take a day trip to Nusa Penida Island (approximately 30 minutes by ferry)
* Visit the famous Crystal Bay and enjoy snorkeling or swimming
* Explore the island's scenic landscapes and beaches
* Return to Ubud in the evening and relax

Day 6: Seminyak

* Drive to Seminyak (approximately 1 hour) and check-in to a local hotel
* Explore the town's trendy boutiques and restaurants
* Visit the nearby Petitenget Temple and enjoy the sunset
* Enjoy a relaxing evening in Seminyak

Day 7: Canggu

* Drive to Canggu (approximately 30 minutes) and explore the town's surf beaches
* Visit the nearby Tanah Lot Temple and enjoy the sunset
* Enjoy a traditional Balinese dinner and watch a cultural performance
* Return to your hotel and relax

Day 8: Bali Highlights Tour

* Take a guided tour to explore Bali's highlights, such as the Tirta Empul Temple and the Goa Gajah Cave
* Visit the nearby villages and try some local cuisine
* Enjoy a relaxing evening in Canggu

Day 9: Beach Day

* Spend the day relaxing on the beach in Canggu or Seminyak
* Enjoy water sports, such as surfing or paddleboarding
* Visit the nearby cafes and restaurants and try some local cuisine
* Enjoy a sunset dinner at a beachfront restaurant

Day 10: Departure

* Spend the morning relaxing at your hotel or exploring the nearby area
* Check-out of your hotel and depart for the airport
* Fly back home

**Estimated Budget:**
The estimated budget for this 10-day trip is:

* Flights: $500 - $700
* Accommodation: $300 - $500
* Food and drink: $200 - $300
* Transportation: $50 - $100
* Activities and entrance fees: $100 - $200
* Total: $1,150 - $1,800

**Travel Tips:**

* Make sure to book your flights and accommodation in advance to avoid high prices.
* Be respectful of Balinese culture and traditions, especially when visiting temples or attending cultural performances.
* Try to learn some basic Indonesian phrases, such as "hello" (halo) and "thank you" (terima kasih).
* Be prepared for crowds and traffic in popular areas, such as Kuta and Seminyak.
* Don't forget to try some local cuisine, such as nasi goreng and satay.
* Stay hydrated and bring sunscreen, as the sun can be strong in Bali.

I hope this helps, and you have a great trip to Bali!
